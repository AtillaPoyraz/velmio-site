Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 14, 2026

Velmio is designed to help users manage pet care information such as pet profiles, reminders, health records, expenses and feeding estimates.

Data Storage

Velmio stores user-created pet care data locally on the user’s device. This may include pet names, photos, notes, reminders, health records and expenses.

Location

Velmio may request location access to show nearby veterinary clinics. Location is used only for this feature and is not sold or shared.

Data Sharing

Velmio does not sell personal data. Velmio does not share user-created pet care data with third parties.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: velmioapp@gmail.com
